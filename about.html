About Shri Someshwara Temple

The Shri Someshwara Temple in Hoskeri, Gokarna, is a sacred shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva worshipped as Someshwara. For generations this temple has been a center for devotion, culture and communal life. The sanctuary's stone architecture, rituals and festivals carry the imprint of centuries of local tradition.

Legends tell of ancient sages and local rulers who protected and patronized the shrine. Over time, the temple became a focal point where folk traditions and Vedic practices harmonize — bhajans, seasonal festivals, and community feasts have preserved a living heritage. Devotees come seeking blessings, peace and renewal.
