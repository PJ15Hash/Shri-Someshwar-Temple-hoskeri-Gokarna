About Shri Someshwara Temple

The Shri Someshwara Temple in Hoskeri, Gokarna, is a sacred shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva (Someshwara). This temple has been a center of devotion and cultural life for generations. The architecture, rituals and festivals reflect a long heritage of local tradition.

Legends mention ancient sages and local patrons who protected the shrine. Over time, this sacred place became a focal point where folk traditions and Vedic practices harmonize – bhajans, seasonal festivals, and community feasts keep the spiritual life vibrant.

Devotees visit seeking blessings, peace and renewal. The temple continues to welcome pilgrims with daily aartis, special poojas, and communal activities.
