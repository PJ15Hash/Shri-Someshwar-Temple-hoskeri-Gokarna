History

The Someshwar Temple in Hoskeri is a centuries-old shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. It has been a center of faith, culture, and community service for generations.

Darshan Timings

Morning: 6 AM – 12 PM
Evening: 4 PM – 9 PM

Daily Rituals

Morning & Evening Aarti, Bhajans, and Special Poojas conducted daily.

Festivals

Rath Yatra, Diwali, Navratri are celebrated with grandeur, involving devotees from surrounding villages.

Event Calendar

  • Jan 14 – Makar Sankranti Special Pooja
  • Mar 21 – Maha Shivratri Celebrations
  • Oct – Navratri & Vijayadashami
  • Nov – Diwali Pooja
